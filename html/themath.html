\(w \Rightarrow \text{width of bay}\)
\(h \Rightarrow \text{height of bay}\)
\(n \Rightarrow \text{number of crosses in bay}\)
\(d \Rightarrow \text{width of dimensional lumber}\)
Solve for:
\(t \Rightarrow \text{angle of brace}\)
\(l \Rightarrow \text{length of brace}\)

\(l=\frac{w}{\cos(t)}\)
\(\tan(x)=\frac{h_{1}}{w}\) AND \(h_{1}+h_{2}=\frac{h}{n}\) OR \(h_{1}=\frac{h}{n}-h_{2}\)
\(\tan(x)=\frac{\frac{h}{n}-h_{2}}{w}\)
\(w\tan(x)=\frac{h}{n}-h_{2}\) AND \(h_{2}=\frac{d}{\cos(t)}\)
\(w\tan(x)=\frac{h}{n}-\frac{d}{\cos(t)}\)
\(w\tan(x)\cos(t)=\frac{h}{n}\cos(t)-d\) AND \(\tan(t)\cos(t)=\sin(t)\)
\(w\sin(x)=\frac{h}{n}\cos(t)-d\)
\(w\sin(t)-\frac{h}{n}\cos(t)=-d\)

\(a\sin(t)-b\cos(t)=c\)
\(R=\sqrt{a^{2}+b^{2}}\)
\(\phi=\tan^{-1}(\frac{b}{a})\)
\(\sin(t-\phi)=\frac{c}{R}\)
\(t=\sin^{-1}(\frac{c}{R})+\phi\)
